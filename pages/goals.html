Our Goals

  • Disseminate environmental awareness and educate the community about the importance of preserving natural resources and promoting responsible environmental behavior.
  • Establish sustainable partnerships with other companies and institutions to develop joint environmental projects, exchange knowledge, and experience.
  • Comply with local and international environmental standards and enhance environmental performance.
  • Implement measures to reduce environmental pollution and negative impacts.
  • Encourage research and innovation in environmental areas and develop innovative solutions.
